Articles & Interviews

Working in ecommerce for the past year, I've had the freedom to work on multiple projects. From interviewing famous sports and music personalities, to a series of pieces on Scottish creatives, here are a selection of some of my favourite interviews and articles I've written for Lyle & Scott.

Anton Haig

A brand with a strong golf history, at Lyle & Scott I've interviewed a number of international golfers. Here I chat with Anton Haig, sponsored by the brand, about his career and his plans for his next world tours. April 2013

Rudimental

The British band of the moment, Rudimental have had an incredible 2013. The boys have smash hits "Feel the Love" and "Not Giving In" under their belts, a debut album which has gone platinum and recently being voted festival act of the summer, I catch up with the four London lads who have taken the world by storm. May 2013


Hudson Mohawke

As part of a series of Articles on Scottish Creatives within the arts, I profile Glasweigan DJ and producer Hudson Hohawke. Starting his career DJing in a vegan club in Glasgow, Hudmo (affectionately refered to by his fans) has collaborated with Kanye West on the eagerly anticipated album "Yeezus" and generates a mass audiences at any gig he performs. September 2013

History of the Brogue

The popular brogue shoe originates in Scotland. As part of a series on mens style, I investigate the history of the brogue. Digital illustration created in Photoshop. September 2013
