Newington Scene

I moved to Newington Green in August 2012, and quickly fell head over heels in love with the whole area. After realising I couldn't find a place for a pub quiz on a Sunday, me and a friend decided we'd start our own blog. Reviewing what's happening in London, specifically Newington Green; we've blogged about new hot-spots from diners to Dalston raves, African food on South London rooftops to gut bust burgers and have created a nice twitter following. Below are a selection of some of my favouite posts for www.newingtonscene.com

Zoe's Ghana Kitchen

Back in May, we were invited to come and taste some Ghanaian food, made by Hackney native Zoe. With a kitchen residency at the incredible Sky Rooms by Tower Bridge for the month of May, we ventured South for the night. We toasted the start of our meal with munched on Benchanin and jollof rice, topped off by chin-chin banoffee pie. THE one for Summer
